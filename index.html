MY PROJECTS

PROTOTYPE CHALLENGE

I participated in a UX Studio Design competition at RIT, where we were challenged to redesign a charity's website. The organization, Water for South Sudan (WFSS), presented their current website, as well as the problems with the design of their current website. My group and I created a prototype for our redesign using Figma. We conducted user testing to improve our design. Once we finished our prototype, we presented our design to a panel of judges. While we did not win the competition, our design received an honorable mention for our design and user testing.
